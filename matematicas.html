Matemáticas en español is a seminar of math talks in Spanish. All Spanish language skill levels are welcome. Informal or undergraduate level talks are encouraged since the goal of the seminar is not necessarily to learn new math concepts, but to practice communicating math in Spanish.

Amadeus Martin and I founded Matemáticas en español in February 2019. We were recognized for our work founding and organizing this seminar with the Ben Nolting Award for work in diversity and inclusion in 2021. I wrote a piece in Spanish about the seminar featured in the Math Values MAA Blog!

Matemáticas en español is taking a break for the summer. Please join the listserv called matematicas for email updates. Since Fall 2021, I have been co-organizing this seminar with Audrey Goodnight. Feel free to email them or me with any questions.
